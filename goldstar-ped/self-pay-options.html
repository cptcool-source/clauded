Additional Services

Some services may incur additional fees including: lab work, in-office procedures (laser, spirometry), vaccine administration, and specialist referral paperwork. We will always discuss costs before proceeding.

Frequently asked questions

Do I need to pay upfront?

Yes, self-pay fees are due at the time of service. We accept cash, credit/debit cards, and major payment apps.

Can I use my HSA or FSA?

Yes, Health Savings Account (HSA) and Flexible Spending Account (FSA) cards are accepted for eligible medical expenses.

What if I can't afford the visit?

Please call us before your appointment. We are committed to working with families on a case-by-case basis. We would rather help you find a solution than have your child go without care.
