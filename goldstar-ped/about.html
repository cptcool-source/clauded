About Gold Star Pediatrics

A practice built on the belief that every child deserves more than the minimum standard.

Our Story

North Port's gold standard in pediatric care.

Dr. Patel founded Gold Star Pediatrics with a clear vision: to bring hospital-grade pediatric care to North Port families who had long been driving to larger cities just to access the equipment and expertise their children needed. For over 13 years, he has been building a practice where state-of-the-art technology and deeply personal care aren't in conflict — they reinforce each other.

We take a holistic approach that treats the whole child, not just the presenting symptom. That means taking time to understand your family's lifestyle, values, and goals — and building a care plan that reflects them. We believe every child is different, and every family deserves to be treated that way.

Our Philosophy

Care that goes beyond the appointment.

Whole-child care

We look at nutrition, environment, development, and family context alongside medical needs. A well visit isn't a checkbox — it's a conversation about everything that shapes your child's health.

Zero-pressure promise

We offer guidance without judgment on vaccines, feeding choices, parenting decisions, and more. You'll never feel pressured or dismissed here. Our job is to inform — yours is to decide.

Affordable access

We believe great pediatric care should be available to every North Port family, regardless of insurance status. Our flexible self-pay options starting at $119 per visit make that possible.

Why Families Choose Us

What sets us apart.

  • Hospital-grade equipment in a neighborhood officePanda Warmer, LightScalpel CO₂ laser, transcutaneous bilirubin meter, and in-office spirometry — technology usually found only in hospitals, available right here in North Port.
  • Bilingual care in English and PortugueseOur team includes bilingual staff fluent in English and Portuguese, so every family can communicate comfortably and confidently.
  • Same-day sick visits when availableWe know illness doesn't follow a schedule. We do our best to see sick children the same day they call, so you're not left waiting when your child needs care most.
  • A personal relationship with your doctorAt Gold Star, you see the same provider every visit. We're small enough to know your child by name, and experienced enough to handle whatever comes up.
