Our Policies

What you need to know.

Appointments

We offer scheduled well visits, sick appointments, and same-day visits when available. Please call as early in the day as possible for same-day sick appointments. We ask that you arrive 5–10 minutes early for scheduled visits to complete any necessary paperwork. If you are running late, please call ahead so we can do our best to accommodate you.

Cancellations & No-Shows

We understand that life happens. If you need to cancel or reschedule, please give us at least 24 hours notice when possible. This allows us to offer the appointment to another family in need. Repeated no-shows without notice may affect your ability to schedule future same-day appointments.

Insurance & Payment

We accept most major insurance plans. Your co-pay is due at the time of service. If you have questions about your specific coverage or whether we are in-network with your plan, please call your insurance provider before your visit — we are happy to provide our NPI number if needed. Self-pay options are available starting at $119 per visit. See our Self Pay Options page for details.

After-Hours & Emergencies

For true medical emergencies, call 911 or go to your nearest emergency room. For urgent after-hours questions, please call our main number (941-266-5629) and follow the prompts for the on-call line. We do our best to respond within a reasonable time. For non-urgent questions, the patient portal is available 24/7 for secure messaging.

Our Vaccine Philosophy

We believe in science-based guidance and a respectful, no-pressure approach to vaccines. We will always provide honest information and our honest recommendations. Ultimately, we respect that these decisions belong to you and your family. We ask only that all conversations remain open and respectful — and that you feel free to ask every question you have. There are no judgment-free zones quite like our office.

Questions about our policies?

We're always happy to clarify anything before your visit. Give us a call or send a message through the patient portal.
