Our People

The faces behind your child's care.

We're a tight-knit team of three — and that's intentional. Every child who comes through our doors knows each of us, and we know them.

Dr. Patel photo
★

Dr. Jignesh Patel, MD

Pediatrician & Founder

Dr. Patel founded Gold Star Pediatrics with a simple belief: North Port families deserve the same quality of pediatric care as families in larger cities — without the long drive. With over 13 years of experience, he combines hospital-grade technology with a genuinely personal approach. He takes time to listen, explain, and partner with you on your child's health journey.

Melissa photo

Melissa

Practice Manager, RN, MSN

With over 20 years of nursing experience and a Master's in Nursing Leadership, Melissa brings a calm, expert presence to every interaction. She manages the practice, coordinates care, and makes sure every family feels welcome from the moment they call to the moment they leave.

Laura photo

Laura Lima De Almeida, PA-C

Physician Assistant

Bilingual in English and Portuguese, Laura brings a rare combination of warmth and clinical expertise. With a background in Human Biology and Biomedical Sciences, she provides comprehensive pediatric care and ensures that every family — regardless of language — feels heard and at home.
