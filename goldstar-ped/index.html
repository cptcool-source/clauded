Newborn Care

Panda Warmer

Usually found only in hospitals and birthing centers, our mobile Panda Warmer keeps newborns safe and warm during their first visit — with integrated vital monitoring and a built-in scale. Your baby gets the same standard of care they'd receive in a NICU, right here in our North Port office.

Tongue & Lip Tie

LightScalpel CO₂ Laser

Our FDA-cleared LightScalpel performs tongue tie and lip tie releases with exceptional precision — minimal bleeding, less swelling, and faster healing than traditional methods. Most children are back to comfortable feeding within hours. The laser is American-made, FDA cleared for soft tissue procedures, and gentle enough for infants.

Jaundice Screening

No-Blood Bilirubin Check

Our transcutaneous bilirubin meter screens for jaundice by scanning your baby's skin — no needles, no blood draw. Checked three times at every newborn visit, it's the same gold standard used at leading hospitals. Accurate, fast, and completely painless for your newborn.

Respiratory Care

In-Office Spirometry

We use an in-office pulmonary function test to measure how well your child's lungs are working — critical for managing asthma, wheezing, and chronic cough. We can test, treat, and retest in a single visit, so you leave knowing the medication is working — not just hoping.
