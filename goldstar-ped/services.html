Our Services

Comprehensive care from newborns through adolescence — all in one North Port office.

What We Offer

Care for every stage of childhood.

From prenatal visits through teenage years, we're your partner in raising a healthy, happy child.

Newborn Care

In-office newborn exams with hospital-grade equipment including a Panda Warmer, transcutaneous bilirubin meter, and built-in vital monitoring — usually found only in hospitals.

Well Child Exams

Yearly physicals, school forms, and vision and hearing screens — all in one place, from infancy through adolescence. We track development at every stage.

ADHD Diagnosis & Treatment

Comprehensive ADHD evaluation, medication management, and ongoing family support — not just a prescription, but a plan tailored to your child's needs.

Laser Procedures

In-office tongue tie and lip tie releases using our FDA-cleared LightScalpel CO₂ laser — gentle, precise, fast-healing. Most children are comfortable again within hours.

Asthma & Respiratory

In-office spirometry, nebulizer treatments, and ongoing management for children with asthma, wheezing, and chronic cough. Test, treat, and retest in a single visit.

Immunizations

We follow your family's lead on vaccines. Guidance without pressure, information without judgment — always. We respect your choices and answer your questions honestly.

Sick Visits

Same-day sick appointments when available. We offer rapid testing for strep, flu, and COVID so you leave with answers — not just a referral. Call early for best availability.

Sports Physicals

School and athletic clearance forms completed same-day. We perform a thorough pre-participation exam and get your child cleared to play as quickly as possible.

Behavioral Health

Developmental screenings, anxiety support, and behavioral health coordination. We connect families with the right specialists and stay involved throughout the process.

What Makes Us Different

Hospital-grade technology.
Neighborhood-level care.

Most pediatric offices in North Port don't have this equipment. We invested in it because your child deserves more than the minimum standard of care. Our Panda Warmer provides newborns with hospital-quality warmth and monitoring during their first visit. Our FDA-cleared LightScalpel CO₂ laser performs tongue and lip tie procedures with precision and minimal recovery. Our transcutaneous bilirubin meter screens for jaundice without a single needle stick. And our in-office spirometry lets us test, treat, and confirm results in a single appointment.

You shouldn't have to drive to Sarasota or Tampa for this level of care. It's here in North Port.
